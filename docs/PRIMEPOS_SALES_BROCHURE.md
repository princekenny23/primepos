**Sell faster. Track smarter. Grow with confidence.**

PrimePOS is a powerful cloud Point-of-Sale solution built for today’s competitive business environment. Whether you run a retail shop, wholesale outlet, bar, restaurant, or pharmacy, PrimePOS gives you the tools to control operations, improve customer service, and increase profitability.

## About PrimePOS
PrimePOS is a cloud-based point-of-sale system that helps businesses manage **sales, inventory, employees, and business reports** from one centralized platform. It combines speed, reliability, and real-time visibility so owners and managers can make smarter decisions every day.

---

## Industries Supported
PrimePOS is designed to adapt to different business models and workflows:

- **Retail Shops** – Faster checkout, stock control, and customer loyalty tracking
- **Wholesale Businesses** – Bulk pricing, customer-specific rates, and high-volume stock movement
- **Bars** – Tab-based selling, quick service workflows, and billing flexibility
- **Restaurants** – Table service support, kitchen coordination, and accurate order tracking
- **Pharmacies** – Medicine-specific inventory management, expiry control, and batch tracking

---

## Core Features
PrimePOS delivers essential capabilities every modern business needs:

- **Fast checkout system** – Processes sales quickly to reduce queues, improve customer experience, and increase transaction volume during peak hours.
- **Barcode scanning** – Speeds up item entry, reduces manual errors, and ensures accurate pricing at checkout.
- **Inventory management** – Tracks stock in real time so you always know what is available, what is moving fast, and what needs replenishment.
- **Multi-payment support (cash, mobile money, bank)** – Accepts different payment methods to improve convenience and reduce lost sales.
- **Receipts and invoicing** – Generates clear receipts and invoices for customer trust, accounting records, and tax compliance.
- **Discounts and promotions** – Runs targeted offers and time-based promotions to attract customers and boost repeat business.
- **Customer management** – Stores customer profiles and purchase history to support loyalty programs and personalized service.
- **Supplier management** – Organizes supplier details, purchasing history, and lead times for better procurement decisions.

---

## Retail & Wholesale Features
Built to support both walk-in and bulk trade operations:

- **Bulk pricing** – Automatically applies quantity-based pricing rules to simplify wholesale sales and protect margins.
- **Customer pricing levels** – Assigns unique pricing tiers (retail, reseller, VIP, institutional) to serve different customer groups accurately.
- **Stock tracking** – Monitors product movement from purchase to sale, helping prevent overstocking and stock loss.
- **Purchase orders** – Creates, tracks, and manages supplier orders so replenishment is timely and controlled.
- **Low stock alerts** – Notifies teams before critical items run out, helping maintain sales continuity.

---

## Bar & Restaurant Features
Optimized for high-speed hospitality environments:

- **Table management** – Assigns and tracks tables in real time for smoother seating, ordering, and turnover.
- **Open tabs** – Keeps active customer tabs open for ongoing orders until checkout, ideal for bars and lounges.
- **Split bills** – Divides bills by person or item to handle group payments quickly and accurately.
- **Waiter order management** – Captures orders by staff member for faster service and clearer accountability.
- **Kitchen order tickets** – Sends structured order tickets to the kitchen to reduce mistakes and speed up preparation.

### Business Impact for Hospitality
- Improve order speed during busy service periods.
- Reduce billing disputes with accurate tab and split-bill records.
- Increase table turnover and customer satisfaction.

---

## Pharmacy Features
Specialized tools for pharmacy operations and compliance-focused control:

- **Medicine inventory tracking** – Maintains accurate medicine quantities by item and unit, reducing stock discrepancies.
- **Expiry date alerts** – Warns staff before products expire to minimize losses and protect patient safety.
- **Batch tracking** – Records medicine batches for traceability, recall management, and regulatory confidence.
- **Fast medicine lookup** – Finds medicines quickly by name, barcode, or code for faster and safer dispensing.

### Business Impact for Pharmacies
- Lower losses from expired stock.
- Better compliance and traceability readiness.
- Faster customer service at the counter.

---

## Employee & Security
Keep operations controlled, transparent, and secure:

- **Cashier accounts** – Gives each cashier a unique login for accountability and clean transaction trails.
- **Role-based permissions** – Restricts access based on job role, protecting sensitive business data.
- **Activity tracking** – Logs key user actions (sales edits, voids, stock changes) to reduce fraud risk.
- **Shift management** – Tracks opening/closing balances, sales by shift, and handover accuracy.

---

## Business Reports
Turn daily transactions into actionable business intelligence:

- **Daily sales reports** – Shows sales totals, payment breakdowns, and peak hours for daily decision-making.
- **Profit and loss** – Highlights revenue versus cost trends to guide pricing and spending strategies.
- **Inventory reports** – Identifies fast-moving, slow-moving, and at-risk stock items.
- **Employee performance reports** – Measures staff productivity, sales contribution, and operational efficiency.
