## What PrimePOS Is

PrimePOS is a multi-business POS and operations platform built for growing businesses that need one system for selling, stock control, customer management, staff operations, reporting, and online ordering.

PrimePOS is best positioned as:

- a modern POS and business operations platform
- a multi-outlet management system
- a restaurant and bar operations platform
- a storefront and WhatsApp ordering system
- an operational reporting system for owners and managers

Do not position PrimePOS as a full accounting ERP. The codebase supports strong operational finance and reporting workflows, but not a full general ledger accounting engine.

## Retail Shop Pitch

### Short pitch

PrimePOS helps retail shops sell faster, track stock in real time, manage returns and discounts, and keep daily operations under control from one system.

### Sales pitch

If you run a retail shop, PrimePOS gives you the tools to handle day-to-day sales without depending on notebooks and disconnected spreadsheets. Your team can process sales quickly, monitor stock levels, manage returns, track customer buying history, and review performance from a clear dashboard. It is designed for businesses that need speed at the till and control behind the scenes.

### Best angle to sell

Speed at checkout plus stronger stock control.

## Video Production Style Guide

### Recommended style

- screen-record product UI clearly
- keep cuts fast and clean
- add large captions for each benefit
- use client industry footage between screen recordings
- show before/after pain and outcome

### Recommended structure

- first 3 seconds: problem or promise
- next 20 to 40 seconds: product workflow
- last 5 seconds: result and CTA

### Caption examples

- Fast checkout
- Real-time stock control
- Customer credit visibility
- Manage tables with clarity
- Open and close tabs faster
- Launch your online storefront
- Track sales with confidence
