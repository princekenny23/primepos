# How to Manage Customers in CRM

## Question This Answers
How do I create customers, update details, and use customer data during sales?

## Add Customer
1. Go to CRM -> Customers.
2. Click Add Customer.
3. Enter name, phone, optional email and address.
4. Save.

## Edit Customer
1. Open customer profile.
2. Update fields.
3. Save changes.

## Use Customer in POS
1. In POS, open customer selector.
2. Search and choose customer.
3. Complete sale.

## Expected Result
- Customer appears in CRM list.
- Customer can be selected in POS.
- Sales can be filtered by customer.

## Best Practices
- Always include phone number for communication.
- Avoid duplicate entries by searching first.
