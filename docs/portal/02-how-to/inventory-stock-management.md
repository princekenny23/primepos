# How to Add and Track Inventory

## Question This Answers
How do I create stock, update stock, and monitor inventory levels?

## Add a New Product
1. Go to Inventory -> Products.
2. Click Add Product.
3. Enter product name, SKU, price, and opening stock.
4. Save.

## Edit Existing Stock
1. Open product from product list.
2. Click Adjust Stock.
3. Choose increase or decrease.
4. Enter quantity and reason.
5. Save.

## Track Inventory Health
1. Open Inventory Dashboard.
2. Review:
- Low Stock
- Expiring Products (if enabled)
- Fast-moving items

## Expected Result
- Product quantity is always current.
- Stock movement log is available for audit.

## Best Practices
- Use SKU format consistently (example: CAT-0001).
- Record a reason for every manual stock adjustment.
- Review low-stock list daily.
