# How to Complete a POS Cash Sale

## Question This Answers
How do I process a normal cash sale from start to finish?

## Steps
1. Open Sales/POS.
2. Search product by name, barcode, or SKU.
3. Add product to cart.
4. Adjust quantity if needed.
5. Confirm subtotal and total.
6. Click Checkout.
7. Select Cash.
8. Enter amount received.
9. Confirm payment.
10. Print or share receipt.

## Expected Result
- Sale is marked completed.
- Receipt number is generated.
- Stock is reduced.

## Tips
- If cart has many items, use cart scroll to review all lines.
- Always confirm outlet before checkout.

## Troubleshooting
- Error: Cart is empty
  - Add at least one product.
- Error: Insufficient stock
  - Reduce quantity or restock item.
