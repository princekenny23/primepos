# Quick Start: First 30 Minutes

## Goal
Complete your first setup and make your first successful sale.

## Before You Start
- You have your login email and password.
- You know your business name, outlet name, and default currency.
- You have at least one product to sell.

## Step 1: Sign In and Confirm Business Profile (3 minutes)
1. Log in to PrimePOS.
2. Open Settings or Business Profile.
3. Confirm business name, contact phone, and currency.
4. Save changes.

Success check:
- Your business name shows correctly on the dashboard header.

## Step 2: Create Your First Product (7 minutes)
1. Go to Inventory -> Products.
2. Click Add Product.
3. Enter:
- Product Name: Example: 1L Cooking Oil
- SKU: Example: OIL-001
- Selling Price
- Opening Stock
4. Save.

Success check:
- Product appears in product list with stock greater than 0.

## Step 3: Make Your First POS Sale (10 minutes)
1. Go to Sales/POS.
2. Search and add your product to cart.
3. Confirm quantity.
4. Click Checkout.
5. Select payment method (cash/card/mobile).
6. Confirm sale.

Success check:
- Receipt number generated.
- Sale appears in sales history.

## Step 4: Verify Stock Reduced (3 minutes)
1. Go back to Inventory -> Products.
2. Find your product.
3. Confirm stock reduced by sold quantity.

Success check:
- Quantity is updated in product list.

## Step 5: Optional E-commerce Quick Start (7 minutes)
1. Go to Storefront settings.
2. Select site or create one.
3. Add product to catalog.
4. Open storefront URL and confirm product is visible.

Success check:
- Product appears on public storefront page.

## Common First-Day Mistakes
- Product has 0 stock: It will not sell online.
- Wrong outlet selected: Sales and stock go to wrong outlet.
- Missing WhatsApp number: Checkout chat links will fail.
