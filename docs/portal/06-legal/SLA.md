# Service Level Agreement (SLA) - PrimePOS SaaS ERP

Effective Date: 2026-04-03

## 1. Scope
This SLA defines service commitments for PrimePOS cloud ERP services including POS, Inventory, CRM, E-commerce, and Fleet modules.

## 2. Service Availability
- Target monthly uptime: 99.5%
- Planned maintenance window: off-peak hours, announced in advance
- Exclusions:
  - Force majeure events
  - Third-party outages outside provider control
  - Customer network/device issues

## 3. Support Response Times
- Critical (system down, payment blocked): first response within 1 hour
- High (major function degraded): first response within 4 hours
- Medium (feature issue/workaround available): first response within 1 business day
- Low (minor issue/how-to requests): first response within 2 business days

## 4. Data Security and Privacy
- Data in transit encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.
- Access controlled with role-based permissions.
- Sensitive credentials must be rotated when exposed.
- Access logs retained according to compliance policy.

## 5. Backup and Recovery
- Backup frequency: daily minimum (recommended more frequent for high-volume tenants)
- Backup retention: configurable by plan
- Disaster recovery objective:
  - RPO target: 24 hours
  - RTO target: 8 hours

## 6. Data Ownership
- Customer retains ownership of business and user data.
- Provider acts as data processor for contracted services.
- Customer may request export in standard machine-readable format, subject to policy.

## 7. Customer Responsibilities
- Maintain secure user passwords and role assignments.
- Report incidents promptly with timestamp and context.
- Ensure endpoint devices and local network are secure.

## 8. Service Credits (Optional Commercial Clause)
If monthly uptime falls below committed threshold, customer may qualify for service credits according to contract terms.

## 9. Changes to SLA
SLA updates will be communicated in writing with effective date and revision notes.
